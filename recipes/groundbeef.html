Ground Beef Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pack of Ground Beef
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Heat a pan at high heat and spray your choice of oil
  2. Lay all of your ground beef evenly across the pans service and let it simmer for 3 minutes
  3. Season ground beef evenly with your choice of seasonings
  4. Flip the ground beef when it is brown in the bottom
  5. Cook for another 5 minutes
  6. Bring the pan off the heat and tilt the pan so all the oil are on one side and ground beef on the other
  7. Scoop the excess oil out using a spoon
  8. Put into plate and serve/store.
