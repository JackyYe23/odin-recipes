Waffle Recipe

Description

Delicious Waffle with fresh berries and other toppings of your desire. Quick and easy and makes for a delightful breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Waffle Iron
  • Waffle Batter
  • Fresh Berries
  • Oil Spray
  • Other Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Spray Oil onto Waffle Iron
  2. Once machine is ready, pour waffle batter on top of Waffle Iron, make sure every side is filled.
  3. Wait for Waffle Iron to beep, signalling its ready.
  4. Carefully take waffle out and top with desired toppings.
