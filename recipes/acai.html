Acai Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

  • Frozen Acai Packets
  • Frozen Berries
  • Bananas
  • Milk
  • Honey
  • Fresh Fruit Toppings
  • Other Toppings

Steps

  1. Add Frozen Acai Pack to Blender by breaking it into small pieces
  2. Add milk and frozen berries
  3. Blend until it is smooth
  4. Put Acai into bowl and top wil toppings.
